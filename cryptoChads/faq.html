Frequently asked Questions (FAQ)

Question?

Does CryptoChads support more fiat currencies than what is listed in the table?


Answer!

Currently we only support what is listed in our currency price value table but we do plan on supporting

more by popular demand.

Question?

Can i buy and sell crypto with CryptoChads?


Answer!

No, we are not a trading platform, just a coin pirce tracker.

Question?

Will CryptoChads implement a crypto currency calculator?


Answer!

It's on the roadmap but deadline is not currently known.

Question?

Will CryptoChads support more crypto currencies?


Answer!

Yes and no, we list only what we are interested in keeping track of. However if the demand is high enough

we might.

Question?

What is CryptoChads?


Answer!

We are a coin price value tracker. We keep track of five different cryptocurrencies and their respective

value in twelve different fiat currencies.

Question?

Does CryptoChads have alternative means of contact other than email?


Answer!

No, we only support email for community communication. You can PGP encrypt your emails to

us using our CryptoChads public PGP key if you require more privacy.
