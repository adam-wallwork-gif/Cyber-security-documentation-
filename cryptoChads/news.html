The current state of cryptocurrency in the UK 2023

(CoinMarketCap, 2023)

Crypto currency in the UK is more and more being restricted and crypto currency ATMs are being banned

(bbc, 2022) under fears of money laundering activity. Crypto currency is supposed to be a unrestricted,

decentralised and sudo anonymous way of transacting without fear of government overeach, restrictions

and regulations. This is why further restricting crypto could be potentially harmful or counter productive.
